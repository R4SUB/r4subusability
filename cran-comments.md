## Submission notes

This is the first submission of r4subusability. It is part of the R4SUB
(R for Regulatory Submission) ecosystem and fills the usability pillar gap
— all six regulatory authority profiles in 'r4subprofile' allocate 15-20%
weight to usability evidence, but no existing package emits it.

All packages listed in Imports are available on CRAN: r4subcore, cli,
rlang, tibble.
